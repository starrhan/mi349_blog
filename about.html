A Lot About Me

My name is Hannah Starr. I will be graduating in Spring 2021 with a Bachelor of Arts in Marketing from the Broad College of Business and a Minor in Information Technology Communication and Development. In December 2021, I will be graduating with a Master of Science in Marketing Research from the Broad College of Business. Starting in the spring, I will be enrolled in all graduate courses.

While in college, I have been very involved on campus. I am a member of Gamma Phi Beta Sorority. I was voted Photography chair and am a member of three sub-committees. I am in the Honors College and Order of Omega, Michigan State University's Greek Honor Society. I am president of Mortar Board and secretary of DECA. I work with TikTok and Microsoft as brand ambassadors and campus representatives.
